Sir Jagdish Chandra Bose

Sir Jagadish Chandra Bose CSI CIE FRS was a biologist, physicist, botanist and an early writer of science fiction

Here's a time line of Sir Jagdish Chandra Bose:

  • 1858 -Bengal Presidency, British India(now in Bangladesh)
  • 1869 -Bose joined the Hare School in 1869 and then St. Xavier's School at Kolkata.
  • 1884-Bose obtain a B.A with a second class in Natural Science tripos from Cambridge
  • 1875 -he passed the Entrance Examination (equivalent to school graduation) of the University of Calcutta and was admitted to St. Xavier's College, Calcutta.
  • 1879 - received a BA from the University of Calcutta
  • 1884 - BSc from the University College London affiliated under University of London
  • 1885 -He was appointed as a Physics Professor at Presidency college , Kolkata.
  • 1894 - Bose ignited gunpowder and rng bell at a distance using microwaves in wave length in millimeter of range and it was held in public demonstration in the Town Hall of Calcutta .
  • 1896 - DSc from the University College London, University of London
  • 1900 Bose wrote "Niruddesher Kahini". It was the first notable work in Bengali Science Fiction
